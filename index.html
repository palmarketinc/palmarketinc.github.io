About Us

Welcome to Your Neighborhood Market on University Ave! We’re a proud, family-owned grocery store located in the heart of Palo Alto. From fresh produce, quality meats, and deli sandwiches to refreshing ice cream, snacks, and drinks — we’ve got something for everyone. Need to get more done in one stop? We also offer check cashing services and on-site laundry for your convenience. Come by and experience friendly service and everything you need — all under one roof!
